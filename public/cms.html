Teach your cat to use a scratching post

When your cat uses a scratching post not only will this help prevent damage to your furniture, it will help your cat stretch her muscles and keep her claws in top condition.

Always keep plenty of fresh water available

Clean, fresh water is essential for your cat's good health. If your cat doesn't seem to drink from a bowl, consider providing her with a tall glass.
